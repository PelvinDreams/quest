Who We Are

Creative Tech Limited is a leading technological company with focus on Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Digital Skill Training, Solar Power Generation, and Computer Sales and Services. Founded in 2015.

Kick started in 2016, Creative Tech Gadget repairs has grown into a nationaly known brand with clients coming from Enugu in the East, Kogi and Benue in the North, Akwa Ibom in the South and Lagos in the west.

Creative Tech Gadget repairs is known for its expertise in servicing and repairs of Hp, Apple, Dell, Lenovo, Samsung and other products. We fix laptops, phones and tablets of different models.

Our trademark for the past four years has been speed, accuracy and cost-effectiveness, in the way we deliver our services.

Creative Tech Solar power service has consistently delivered solar product designed to supply clients and give them long-term peace of mind.

We have a diversified portifolio of executed jobs covering residential, commercial and utility solar energy needs.

Regardless of the size of power generation requested, we've made sure to delivr our promise of durability and efficiency.
